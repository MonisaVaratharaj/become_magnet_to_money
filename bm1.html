Discover Your Ikigai

Here, I will help you discover your Ikigai. In my next blog, I will suggest a simple game on how to convert your Ikigai into an income source.

Answer the following questions truthfully to yourself:

  • Do you really like your current job?
  • Are you getting positive energy and spirit when you do your job?
  • Are you really loving your job? (Loving means you're ready to do your job even for a whole single day - 24 hours)
  • What would you do if money was not your consideration?
  • What do you love to do the most?
  • What do you love doing in your free time?
  • What do you love to talk about the most?
  • What would you do if there was no need to earn money for the rest of your life?
  • What would you do if there were no financial problems or family problems?
  • What would you do if there were no limitations, no barriers, no restrictions? You are completely free to do whatever you want. Then, what would be your choice?

Whether you play cricket, tennis, read books, watch movies, edit photographs and videos, sing songs, or dance… whatever it is, that’s your Ikigai. Yes, even watching movies can be your Ikigai.
